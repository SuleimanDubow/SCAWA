ABOUT US

SCAWA (Somali Children and Women Advocacy) is committed to advocating for the rights and welfare of women and children in Somalia. We work tirelessly to improve their lives through education, healthcare, and empowerment programs. Our mission is to create a safe, healthy, and equitable environment for the most vulnerable communities.


We believe in the power of community-driven solutions and work closely with local stakeholders to ensure our initiatives are sustainable and impactful. Through our efforts, we aim to eradicate poverty, provide access to quality education and healthcare, and empower women and children to achieve their full potential.


Founded on the principles of compassion, integrity, and dedication, SCAWA has been at the forefront of social change in Somalia. Our dedicated team of professionals and volunteers works on the ground to deliver programs that address immediate needs and create long-term solutions. We collaborate with international partners, local organizations, and community leaders to maximize our impact and reach.


Our impact is measured not only by the number of people we reach but by the lasting changes we create in their lives. We are proud of our achievements and remain dedicated to expanding our efforts to reach even more communities in need.


Join us in our mission to create a brighter future for women and children in Somalia. Together, we can make a difference.

Our programs focus on empowering women through education, vocational training, and health services. We strive to ensure that women have the tools and resources they need to lead independent and fulfilling lives.

We provide children with access to quality education, healthcare, and protection services. Our goal is to create a nurturing environment where every child can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our community outreach programs aim to raise awareness about critical issues affecting women and children. We engage with local leaders and communities to drive positive change and ensure our initiatives are aligned with their needs.

Through our various programs and initiatives, we have touched the lives of countless women and children in Somalia. From providing education and healthcare to fostering empowerment and protection, our impact is both profound and far-reaching.

There are many ways to get involved with SCAWA. Whether through volunteering, donating, or advocating for our cause, your support is invaluable. Join us in making a difference in the lives of women and children in Somalia.

OUR SERVICES

  1. Raise Fund for Healthy Food

    We aim to combat malnutrition by raising funds to provide healthy and nutritious food to those in need, ensuring that every child and woman has access to balanced meals.

  2. Education for Poor Children

    Our educational programs focus on providing access to quality education for underprivileged children, helping them to achieve their academic goals and break the cycle of poverty.

  3. Promoting the Rights of Children

    We advocate for the rights of children, ensuring they are protected from abuse and exploitation, and have access to opportunities that allow them to thrive.

  4. Massive Donation to the Poor

    We organize donation drives to collect essential items such as clothing, food, and school supplies, distributing them to families and individuals in need.

  5. Huge Help to Homeless Pupils

    We provide support to homeless children, offering them safe shelter, educational resources, and emotional support to help them rebuild their lives.

  1. Let's Build Some Homes

    Our housing projects focus on building safe and affordable homes for families, creating stable living environments where children can grow and thrive.

  2. Pure Water for Poor People

    Access to clean water is a basic human right. We work on providing pure water to communities lacking this essential resource, improving their health and quality of life.

  3. Medical Facilities

    We offer medical services and facilities to ensure that everyone, especially women and children, receives the healthcare they need, including vaccinations, check-ups, and emergency care.

  4. Women's Health and Empowerment

    We focus on women's health by providing access to reproductive health services, maternal care, and empowerment programs that equip women with the skills and knowledge to lead healthier, more independent lives.

  5. Support for Single Mothers

    We offer support programs for single mothers, helping them with childcare, job training, and financial assistance, ensuring they have the resources needed to provide for their families.

SMART CHARITABLES

Somali Children and Women Advocacy (SCAWA) is an independent, non-governmental, non-profit and non political organization established to advocate for the protection of human rights, save and improve lives of most vulnerable, poorest and marginalized families affected by poverty and/or crisis, eradicate poverty and empower women and children to reach their full potential.
